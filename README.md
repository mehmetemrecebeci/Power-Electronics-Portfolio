# Power Electronics Portfolio

This repository contains academic and personal engineering projects related to:

- Power Electronics
- Motor Control
- PCB Design
- Electric Vehicles
- Renewable Energy Systems

## Projects

### 1. Grid Connected Three Phase Inverter
SRF-PLL, dq current control, active/reactive power control and SVPWM analysis.

### 2. Nissan Leaf Referenced IPM Motor Design
IPM traction motor design and FOC based control analysis using Altair FluxMotor and PSIM.

### 3. Interleaved Totem-Pole PFC Converter
3.3 kW closed-loop PFC converter design and analysis.

### 4. Two Switch Forward Converter
389 VDC to 24 V isolated converter design and closed-loop control analysis.

### 5. Closed Loop Buck Converter
48 W DC-DC buck converter design and compensation analysis.
